How to make roasted chickpeas....

These are good for protein and also when you want chips but you don't want to eat chips

They always take longer than I think they will

Ingredients

  • chickpeas
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. drain chickpeas but don't rinse them because you're lazy like that
  2. put the chickpeas in the airfryer and spray oil on them
  3. add salt
  4. cook for like 15 minutes
  5. bit of lemon juice always does the trick
